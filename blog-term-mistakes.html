Term Insurance Mistakes to Avoid (Save Lakhs in 2026)

Term insurance is simple — but small mistakes can cost your family lakhs in the future. Avoid these common errors before buying your policy.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Choosing very low coverage
  • Not adding riders (critical illness, accident)
  • Hiding medical history
  • Buying only based on low premium

💡 Smart Tips

  • Take coverage 10–15x your income
  • Compare multiple insurers
  • Check claim settlement ratio
  • Buy early to get lower premium

📌 Why It Matters

A wrong decision today can leave your family financially unprotected. Always make informed choices.
