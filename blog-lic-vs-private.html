LIC vs Private Insurance – Full Comparison (2026)

Confused between LIC and private insurance companies? This is one of the most common questions in India.

LIC (Government Insurance)

  • Backed by Government of India
  • High trust factor
  • Stable long-term plans

Private Insurance Companies

  • Better returns in some plans
  • Flexible policy options
  • Faster online services

Which is Better?

LIC is best for safety and trust, while private insurance offers flexibility and modern features.
